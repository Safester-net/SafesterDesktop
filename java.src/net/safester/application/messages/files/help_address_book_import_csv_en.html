Importing Contacts

Importing contacts from a CSV file

These are the expected characteristics of the CSV file:
  • For each line, the file must contain at least 4 or 5 fields in the following order:
    • 4 fields: Email address, Full Name, Company, Cell phone number.
    • 5 fields: Email Address, Last Name, First Name, Company, Cell phone number.
  • Last Name, First Name and Company fields may be empty. You can choose the order of insertion of last names and first names.
  • Fields after the fifth separator are ignored.
  • The cell phone number format is not imposed, it may be corrected for an international format, or for the French format of 10 digits with a zero as header.
  • Invalid records in the file are ignored and no error message is displayed.

Examples of valid CSV content:
jean.martin@societe.com; Jean Martin;;+33 6 23 25 34 55

nicolas.dupont@yahoo.com; Nicolas Dupont; Transports Parisiens; 33 (0)627559966; 01 45 67 99 00; 6, rue Saint-Lazare; Paris; 75009
