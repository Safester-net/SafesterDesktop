2FA Settings
  • Install and open on your mobile phone a 2FA App that supports HOTP/TOTP standards.
    (andOTP, FreeOTP, Google Authenticator, LastPass Authenticator, etc.).

  • On Safester Desktop, go to 2FA Menu ==> Account QR Code.
    This will create your 2FA account if it not already exists, and will then display the QR Code to scan.

  • Scan the displayed QR Code with your mobile phone 2FA App.

  • Close the Account QR Code Window when the scan is done.

  • The 2FA Activation Status Window will allow you to enable/disable the 2FA code validation entry for next logins on Safester.

Warnings

  • Do not enable 2FA if you have not scanned the QR Code with your 2FA mobile App: you could not anymore connect and authenticate on Safester.

  • We recommend using a 2FA App that supports backup of accounts info.

  • Make sure to backup your mobile 2FA Safester accounts info.
