Welcome to Safester!


Safester is the easy to use, easy to share, secured messaging solution.

To send a confidential email to one of your contact, just compose a message to his regular email address.
If he is not yet registered in Safester system he will receive an invitation. He will get your message as soon as he registers to Safester.
