These settings allow you to configure a Passphrase Hint to help you remember your passphrase, or a Question and associated Answer used to recover your passphrase.

If you choose to enable Passphrase Recovery, your passphrase will be stored encrypted by a 2048 bits OpenPGP key on Safester servers.If you choose to enable Passphrase Hint, your passphrase will NOT be stored on Safester servers. Consider Passphrase Hint as public information.

As a matter of principle we do not recommend the Passphrase Recovery option.
